Caveat for 1D Helios simulations. This diagram visualizes the 3D geometric coupling that fixed-pointing NIF beams see as the capsule implodes. Helios 1D spherical geometry is forced to couple 100% by symmetry regardless of R — it physically cannot reproduce this late-time miss effect. The gap between Helios absorption (~93%) and reference codes (LILAC/HYDRA/Xrage ~65%) on Olson PDD is consistent with exactly this missing physics. Drag R from 0.20 down to 0.08 with the Olson preset active to see the coupling fraction fall dramatically — that is the 3D late-time loss that a 1D code cannot reproduce.
